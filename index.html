My story

I started coding when I was 9 years old when I always wanted to make my own website. At that time, I was using website builders like Weebly and Wix, but those had a lot of paid features.

So I thought that companies like Microsoft definitely didn't use website builders like this, and they could just add any component to their website without paying. Then I looked up how I could do that, when I figured out that they code their websites using HTML and CSS.

Now, years later, I am a web developer who now learns more programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, and even making Roblox games with Lua.
